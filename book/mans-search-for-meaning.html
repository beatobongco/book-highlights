"He who has a Why to live for can bear almost any How." - Friedrich Nietzsche

Frankl shares his experience being a prisoner in a concentration camp. He notes that the ones who survived were not the strongest, but had found the strongest meaning in their existence and suffering. He develops the school of psychotherapy called logotherapy.
